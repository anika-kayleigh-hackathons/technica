About

Many dog shelters struggle from overpopulation and the dog shelters that struggle with overpopulation of dogs are forced to euthanize stray dogs as they are unable to care for them. Shelters nearby may have vaccines available, but overpopulated shelters have no way of knowing because there is no communication between local dog shelters. Help us help shelters collaborate to ensure that all dogs have a home.
